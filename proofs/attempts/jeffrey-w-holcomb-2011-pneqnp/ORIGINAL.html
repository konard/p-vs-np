Jeffrey W. Holcomb (2011) - P≠NP Original Papers

Status: Original papers are no longer accessible.

The original proof papers by Jeffrey W. Holcomb were hosted at:

  • http://www.holcombtechnologies.com (no longer accessible)

According to Woeginger's P versus NP page, the proof sequence included a key paper titled "Just How Random Are Your Answers?" and relied upon the existence of stochastic answers in the set difference between NP and P.

For a reconstruction of the proof argument based on available information, see ORIGINAL.md.
